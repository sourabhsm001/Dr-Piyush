About The Doctor

Healing minds with science & compassion

Dr. Piyush Verma is a highly qualified Consultant Neuropsychiatrist and De-Addiction Specialist based in Dehradun. After completing his M.D, he served as a Senior Resident at AIIMS, Rishikesh — one of India's most prestigious medical institutions.

What sets Dr. Verma apart is his integrated approach: combining modern psychiatric treatment with certified yoga therapy techniques. He believes in treating the whole person — mind, body, and lifestyle — not just symptoms.

He practices at City Heart Centre, Dehradun, where patients describe him as warm, unhurried, and genuinely attentive.
